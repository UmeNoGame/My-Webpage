A little bit more about me

Hello, I am Garance, 25 years old and born in France. My path so far has been anything but linear: I studied law, explored publishing and community service. Each of these chapters has taught me something unique‚Äîdiscipline, adaptability, communication, and creativity. Today, I'm channeling this eclectic background into a new journey: a career in computer science. Technology excites me because it combines logic and problem-solving with innovation and creativity, and I'm eager to grow in this field.

Animals and sports have always been an essential part of my life. I grew up riding horses and spent my childhood in competition, even reaching the French Championship in show jumping, where I proudly earned third place. Beyond equestrian sports, I practice Yoseikan Budo, cycling, and running, all of which give me resilience, focus, and the drive to push beyond limits. My loyal Shiba, Ume, is also by my side, a daily reminder of curiosity, joy, and companionship. I also have two cats: Ninja and Flocon.

Cooking and pastry are another passion of mine‚Äîmore than just a creative outlet, they are a way to bring joy to the people I love. For me, they are about learning precision, embracing patience, and celebrating an essential part of French culture: sharing good food, living fully, and creating warm moments with others.

Reading is another source of inspiration for me. For several years, I ran a literary blog on Instagram, where I wrote reviews and collaborated with publishing houses on partnerships. It was an enriching experience that combined creativity, discipline, and communication, and it deepened my love for stories and culture.

I see myself as dynamic, curious, and open-minded, always motivated to learn from others while contributing my own energy and skills. Whether through technology, teamwork, or cultural exchange. I'm passionate about learning, discovering new cultures, and finding creative ways to connect with people.
